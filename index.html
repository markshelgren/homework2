About Me

Mark Shelgren was born in Buffalo, New York many moons ago. Mark has a B.A. degree in Philosophy from MacMurray College and an MBA degree from Northern Illinois University. Mark currently leads a small software services company specializing in SAP. SAP is a business transaction processing system with its own programming language, ABAP. SAP is used mostly by larger, multi-national organizations.

Mark plays handball and golf, enjoys yoga, anything at the beach and drinking beer. Mark has lived all over the United States and currently resides in Foothill Ranch, CA. Mark and his family lived in France for a year when Mark was in high school. Consequently Mark reads and speaks french fluently. He wishes he was fluent in javascript.

Mark has been married 36 years to his wife Debbie. They have two daughters and three grand children, all male. Mark thinks all three of his grandson's should be taking the Full Stack Development Bootcamp.
